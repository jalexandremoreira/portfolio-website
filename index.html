About

My name is Alexandre Moreira, I am a frontend developer from Portugal, living in Sweden.

After a decade working in the Music Industry as a musician, producer and engineer, I decided to pivot to web development. With the help and encouragement of many friends and acquaintances who work in the industry, I started studying JavaScript ES6, HTML and CSS, completing an online “Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp” by Colt Steele, as well as “JavaScript for Beginners” and “Object-oriented Programming in JavaScript” by Mosh Hamedani. I quickly became aware of the industry’s preference for React.js, and so decided to do a course called “Mastering React”, also by Mosh Hamedani. It quickly became my prefered way of working, and it allowed me the possibility of working with large libraries of data and information that can be effortless and dynamically displayed. I started making websites for friends and joined a web and app development startup called Dinsdag.se, who are currently working with health care entities.
